* 使用Rum
- 添加依赖 ~[rum "0.6.0"]~
- ~(require '[rum.core :as rum])~
简单的定义组件,初始化,并挂载到页面上.
#+BEGIN_SRC clojure
  (ns example
    (:require [rum.core :as rum]))

  (rum/defc label [n text]
    [:.label (repeat n text)])

  (rum/mount (label 5 "abc") js/document.body)
#+END_SRC
